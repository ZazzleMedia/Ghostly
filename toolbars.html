Toolbars

Toolbars Explained

Toolbars are quite custom in that they have very set styles and that they will change the look of dropdowns to suit the look of the actual toolbar. If you need to customise a toolbar then you may overwrite styles, customise the mixin for this one or build your own one. If you do I suggest you use the .inline-nav() mixin as a base thats what I use to get a basic toolbar look.
